JAMJAR [jam•jar]

(noun)

  1. Collection of auto-stitched crowdsourced concert videos.
  2. Tool that lets you watch concert performances from anywhere in the crowd, from the balcony to the pits.
  3. I wanna be the very best, like no one ever was. To collect them is my real test, to stitch them is my cause ~
  4. A Jar of Jams :).
  5. A Drexel University Senior Design Project, created by 5 dope Computer Science students.
